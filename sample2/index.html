Your Guide to
Lasting Hair Removal

Electrolysis is a proven method of permanent hair removal approved by the FDA in the United States. This procedure involves the insertion of a fine needle into the hair follicle, followed by the application of a small amount of electrical current to destroy the follicle’s ability to produce hair. Over time and with consistent treatment sessions, electrolysis effectively eliminates unwanted hair, providing long-lasting results and boosting self-confidence.
